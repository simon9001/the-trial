Early Life & Education

Sheila was born and raised in Kenya, where her bright smile and gentle heart warmed every space she entered. She valued education, compassion, and family above all else.

Her Journey

Sheila lived her life with kindness and strength — uplifting others, nurturing friendships, and spreading joy wherever she went. Her gentle spirit inspired those around her.

Her Memory

Though she is no longer with us in person, her laughter, her generosity, and her faith continue to shine brightly in the lives she touched.

Legacy
