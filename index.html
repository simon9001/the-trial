LIFE STORY

BIRTH
Jacob Mwanthi King'oo was born on 24th March 1995 in Ngilani Village, Mungyani Sub-location, Mungyani Location, Mbitini Division, Makueni County. He was the second-born child of Pastor Shemaiah King'oo Mbai and Justina King'oo. He was a brother to Jemimah Nzembi, Caleb Munyao, Irene Waeni, and an uncle to Nathaniel Taji.

He was a nephew to Nicholas Kioko, Chief Esther Maingi, David Kivindu, James Waema, Solomon Nyamai, Geoffrey Kimeu, and Pastor Festus Mwini. On the maternal side, he was a nephew to the late Muli Kitundu, the late George, the late Joshua, John Munyalo, Alice Muia, Albina Ndumi, Justus Mulwa, Julius Masila, and Josephine Nzula.

He was a grandson to the late Peter Mbai Mutungi, Ruth Mbai, the late Stephen Kitundu, rabecca kitindu Antony Mutungi, and Richard Mutungi.

He was a cousin to Michael Kyavi, Mwendwa, Brian Kyeni, Syombua, Faith, Muuo, Mbatha, Ndanu, Ndungwa, Dan, Esther, Mutanu, Kitonga, Sam, Success, Abraham Kimundu, Festus Mambo, Bernice Mumo, Kennedy Musau, mercy nyamasyo Faith Ndungwa, Dennis Kioko, Catherine Mumo, Moses Mbithi, Precedes among many others.
Jacob was a great-grandson of Joseph Mutiso Ngesu.

EDUCATION

Jacob Mwanthi King'oo joined Kithembeoni Primary School in 2000 and completed his primary education in 2010. He proceeded to Masokani Secondary School in 2011 and completed his secondary education in 2014.
He joined the University of Embu in 2016 and graduated with a Bachelor of Education (Science) in 2019.

CHURCH LIFE

Jacob Kingâ€™oo was born and raised in the Redeemed Gospel Church, Masokani (later renamed Redeemed Gospel Church Milimani). From an early age, he exhibited a consistent and commendable Christian life. during his teenage years, In 2010, he accepted Jesus Christ as his personal Savior and was subsequently baptized.

CHURCH RESPONSIBILITIES
Jacob faithfully served in his local church in various capacities, including Sunday School teacher, Youth Leader, Youth Chairman, and Youth Pastor. Beyond the local church, he also served as the Local Area Youth Secretary, Milimani Sub-Region Youth Treasurer, Milimani Sub-Region Pastorsâ€™ Children Fellowship Coordinator, and as a committee member of the Redeemed Gospel Church, Makueni Region Youth Council. He played a significant role in mobilizing resources and members for local church projects. In addition, he served diligently as a devoted evangelist, preaching the Gospel of Christ in many parts of Kenya. The entire church and the wider Christian community will miss him dearly.

DEMISE
Jacob met his untimely death in a tragic road accident on 31st January 2026, during the KUPPET Nairobi Branch Elections held at City Comprehensive School. As we say farewell, we find comfort in the legacy Jacob leaves behind. He will be deeply missed by his family, friends, colleagues, students and all whose lives he touched. May he rest in eternal peace.
